Lows for protection

Many nations have laws that protect conservation-reliant species: for example, forbidding hunting, restricting land development or creating preserves. Population numbers, trends and species' conservation status can be found in the lists of organisms by population.

Animals

Animals are eukaryotic, multicellular organisms that form the biological kingdom Animalia.animals are motile, heterotrophic, reproduce sexually, and their embryonic development includes a blastula stage.

Reptiles

Reptiles are tetrapod animals in the class Reptilia, comprising today's turtles, crocodilians, snakes, amphisbaenians, lizards, tuatara, and their extinct relatives.

Birds

Birds are a group of endothermic vertebrates,characterised by feathers, toothless beaked jaws,laying eggs,high metabolic rate,four-chambered heart.

Insects

Insects are by far the largest group of hexapod invertebrates within the arthropod phylum.Insects comprise a class within the Phylum Arthropoda.
